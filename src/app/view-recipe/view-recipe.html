Ingredients Needed
  1. Chicken thighs
  2. Mango, diced
  3. Red onion, finely chopped
  4. Cilantro, chopped
  5. Lime juice
  6. Jalapeño, minced
  7. Salt and pepper to taste
  8. Cooked rice for serving
Cooking Instruction
  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill or bake chicken until fully cooked.
  3. Mix mango, onion, cilantro, jalapeño, and lime juice.
  4. Dice chicken and combine with mango salsa.
  5. Serve over cooked rice.
